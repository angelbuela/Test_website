Health and Safety

At Skole, we make your child’s health and safety our top priority. Our standards are the highest in the industry, meeting or exceeding all local and state guidelines. All of our early education and preschool centers include:

  • Supervision to keep children within sight and sound
  • Secured entrances to all centers
  • Nightly cleaning of all surfaces including toys and food spaces
  • Strict hand-washing and sanitary policies to prevent the spread of germs
  • Curriculum that emphasizes healthy practices for children
